Yifan Hang

As a visual artist, I work across various mediums including photography, moving images, computer programming, and installations. With a research background in traditional image-making, my practice seeks to challenge conventional modes of viewing and showcase diverse perspectives via computational techniques. Currently, I am exploring the creative possibilities of digital technology in visual expression, especially for the image.

I usually draw inspiration from everyday objects that are easily overlooked and interact with them to find their intrinsic value. In most of my works, I question the privilege of humans and try to reconstruct the narrative between individual and their surroundings in an interactive way.
